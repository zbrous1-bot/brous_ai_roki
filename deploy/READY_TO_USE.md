# Roki — Ready to Use

This is the final, focused version of the app.

## Primary Purpose
Search for movies and TV shows and quickly see where they are available to stream, rent, or watch for free.

## How to Get It Running (2 minutes)

1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the entire `deploy` folder onto the page.
3. Copy the link.
4. Open it on your phone.
5. Add your TMDB API key and check your services in Settings.

## What’s Included
- Real search with TMDB data
- Clear Streaming / Rent / Buy / Free sections
- Highlighting for services you subscribe to
- "My services only" filter
- Price Tracker (manually log what you pay to rent or buy)
- IMDB and Rotten Tomatoes links
- Optional Roku control (collapsed by default)
